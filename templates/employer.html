Frequently Asked Questions
How do I access my AmbitionBox employer dashboard?
AmbitionBox employer dashboard is only accessible to engaged employers. You can access your account by clicking on this link, verify your email address with a one-time password, and then click on the submit button.
If a company has not claimed page on AmbitionBox, can users still review it?
Yes, a user can write reviews and interviews about a company, irrespective of the fact whether the company is registered on AmbitionBox or not.
Can an employer request to get its profile removed from the site?
No, a company profile cannot be removed from our website. You can, however, report of any discrepancies in your company page by reaching out to us at support@ambitionbox.com. Furthermore, if you are concerned about certain factually incorrect reviews about your company, you can report the same using the 'Report' feature below each review. Kindly mention the reason for reporting and provide relevant evidence to support the claim.
How to post jobs on AmbitionBox?
You cannot post jobs on AmbitionBox. Please note that the jobs displayed on our website are aggregated from our associated job sites like Naukri.com, iimjobs.com, hirist.com.
How to download reviews or salaries data in consolidated format?
The concerned data cannot be provided to you as a downloadable file format as per our privacy policy.
Can multiple users access the AmbitionBox employer dashboard?
Yes, you can invite additional team members to manage your company page on AmbitionBox through the dashboard.
How is the authenticity of reviews checked?
  • Our proprietary algorithm filters and analyses multiple attributes of the review - spam, fake, abusive, duplicate and similar factors to decide the authenticity of a review.
  • Any reported or flagged content is reviewed by a team of moderators and is removed if it doesn't meet our community guidelines.
What can you do to improve your rating?
  • Have sufficient and latest reviews: Having large number of reviews ensures that actual company culture is reflected and not a biased opinion. Latest reviews will reflect the impact of actions you may have taken in the recent past.
  • Respond to reviews: Respond regularly to reviews shared by your employees. This increases trust among employees and they feel valued, resulting in higher ratings eventually.
  • Report reviews: You can report fake/spam/inappropriate reviews directly from the website. Our support team will look into those reviews on priority and take action.
  • Take feedback constructively: Ratings and reviews can be taken as an opportunity to improve areas that the company is lacking in.
How some of our published reviews are removed from AmbitionBox?

We can take down reviews in certain cases where we feel the platform is being compromised:

  • When the reviewer asks us to delete the review.
  • In case it does not meet our community guidelines.
  • If we receive valid evidence or reason, proving that the review is wrong.
  • If we find a user misusing the platform in any way.
Why my competitor's reviews are better than mine?

There could be several reasons that your competitor's reviews are better than yours. For example, the number of your reviews for your company might be very less. Less number of reviews may not present a clear picture of the company.

In order to up your game, encourage your current and former employees to leave reviews for your company. This can help you get better ratings. However, please keep in mind that compensating or coercing employees into leaving a biased review is against our community guidelines. The reviews will be immediately removed in such a case.

Additionally, you are advised to take the negative reviews and ratings constructively and analyze the parameters that you are lacking on. For instance, if your company generally receives lower ratings on work-life balance section, maybe it is time to take care of this aspect.

Over time we believe as you start getting more reviews, the overall rating would give a better picture of your organization.

Can an employer request for the identity of a reviewer?
It is at the user's discretion to submit a review anonymously or with details such as name, photo, designation. The employer will be able to view only the information that is publicly available on our website. By no means, the details of the users other than that are visible publicly will be disclosed by us. Refer to our Terms and Conditions for further details.
